1. Rental Eligibility

Users must be 18+ with valid identification to rent or list an item. Verification may be required before approval.

2. Item Condition

All items must be in working condition and accurately described. Any defects must be clearly mentioned by the owner.

3. Rental Duration & Pricing

Prices are set by the owner and may vary. Rental periods are agreed upon before checkout. Late returns may incur penalties.

4. Return Policy

Items must be returned in the same condition. Damages will be evaluated and may be charged to the renter accordingly.

5. Cancellations & Refunds

Refunds are subject to the owner's policy. If a listing is canceled by the owner, a full refund will be processed within 3–5 business days.

6. Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy. Your personal information is never shared with third parties except as required to process orders or comply with the law.

7. Contact

For any disputes, queries, or complaints, reach out to us at support@gareebi.com. We’ll get back within 24 hours.
